About Aaganshiksha

Our Mission

Aaganshiksha website ka maqsad Aganwadi centres se jude students ke parents, teachers aur admin ke liye ek centralized digital platform provide karna hai. We aim to enhance the quality of early childhood education by providing tools for better administration, communication, and monitoring.

Our Vision

Creating a network of digitally connected Aganwadi centers that provide excellence in early childhood development across India. We envision a future where every child has access to quality early education, nutrition, and care through a well-managed Aganwadi system.

Our Objectives

  • To provide a centralized platform for managing Aganwadi operations
  • To improve communication between parents, teachers, and administrators
  • To facilitate better tracking of student attendance and development
  • To ensure proper management of resources and inventory
  • To share educational content, nutritional tips, and activities
  • To enable data-driven decision making through reports and analytics

Our History

Aaganshiksha was founded with the vision of bringing digital transformation to the Aganwadi ecosystem. Recognizing the challenges faced by these centers in terms of resource management, attendance tracking, and communication, we developed a comprehensive platform to address these issues.

Our journey began with extensive research and consultations with Aganwadi workers, administrators, and parents to understand their specific needs and challenges. This led to the development of a user-friendly, feature-rich platform designed specifically for the Aganwadi context.
